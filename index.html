Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

What is Opinion Kings?

Opinion Kings is a social prediction market where you trade on real-world events — politics, crypto, sports, culture, and more. Think of it as a stock market for opinions, powered by community conviction.

Is this gambling?

No. These are event-based financial contracts structured within regulatory frameworks. Opinion Kings operates under a compliance-first, U.S.-regulated structure with AML/KYC protocols built in from day one.

When does it launch?

Beta access begins with early waitlist members. The earlier you join and the higher you rank, the sooner you get inside. Founding Trader window is limited to the first 500 signups.

How do I move up the waitlist?

Invite friends using your referral link. 1 referral = $5 credit. 5 refs = Priority Beta Seat. 10 refs = VIP Founder (0% fees 90 days). 25+ refs unlock cash bonuses. Max credit cap: $50 (10 refs).

What markets can I trade?

At launch, 14+ markets across crypto, politics, macro economics, sports, AI, and culture. New markets added weekly based on community demand and trending events.

What are the fees?

2% contract fee on winning trades. Zero fees during beta. Founding Traders (first 500 signups) lock in a 30-day zero-fee period from their first trade, plus reduced rates permanently.
